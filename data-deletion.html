Data Deletion
Instructions

Sowmitras Technosoft Solutions respects your right to privacy and digital autonomy. This document outlines the explicit steps required to permanently delete your account data and revoke our access to your connected social profiles.

01 Introduction & Your Rights

In compliance with global data protection regulations (including GDPR and CCPA) as well as the platform compliance guidelines of our integration partners (such as Meta and Instagram), users maintain the absolute right to request the deletion of their personal information from our servers. Once a valid deletion request is processed, your data will be permanently purged from our active databases.

02 Direct Data Deletion Request

To request the complete deletion of your account and all associated data maintained by Sowmitras Technosoft Solutions, please follow these steps:

  • Submit a Request: Send an email to support@sowmitras.com from the email address associated with your account.
  • Subject Line: Please use the exact subject line: "Data Deletion Request - [Your Name/Company Name]".
  • Verification: Our administrative team will reply within 48 hours to securely verify your identity before proceeding with the destructive action.
  • Confirmation: Once verified, your data will be securely and permanently deleted from our active systems within 15 to 30 business days. You will receive a final confirmation email when the process is complete.

03 Revoking Meta & Instagram Access

Because our application integrates with Instagram and Meta APIs to provide analytical and business management tools, you can also independently sever our application's access to your social profiles directly through your Meta settings. This will immediately stop any future data synchronization.

To remove our application via Facebook / Meta:

  • Log in to your Facebook account and navigate to Settings & Privacy > Settings.
  • In the left-hand menu, scroll down and click on Apps and Websites.
  • Locate Sowmitras Technosoft Solutions in the list of active apps.
  • Click Remove and confirm your choice in the popup window.

To remove our application via Instagram:

  • Open the Instagram app and navigate to your profile.
  • Tap the menu icon (three lines) in the top right, then select Settings and privacy.
  • Tap on Website permissions, then select Apps and websites.
  • Find Sowmitras Technosoft Solutions under the "Active" tab and tap Remove.

Note: Removing the app from your Meta/Instagram settings prevents us from accessing new data, but it does not automatically delete previously collected data stored on our servers. To ensure complete removal of historical data, please complete the Direct Data Deletion Request (Section 02).

04 Data Retention Exceptions

While we strictly honor data deletion requests, please be aware that certain data subsets may be temporarily retained due to inescapable legal, security, or financial compliance obligations:

  • Financial Records: Transaction histories and invoices may be retained for up to 7 years to comply with local tax and accounting laws.
  • Security Logs: Anonymized server logs may be retained briefly to detect and prevent fraud, network attacks, or abuse of our services.
  • Legal Holds: Data subject to a valid legal subpoena or investigation cannot be deleted until the legal hold is formally lifted.

05 Contact Support

If you require assistance navigating the data deletion process or have concerns regarding how your data is being handled, our data governance team is available to help.
