Sowmitras Technosoft Solutions Privacy Policy

At Sowmitras Technosoft Solutions, protecting your data and ensuring transparency is our priority. This document outlines our corporate practices regarding the collection, management, and protection of your personal information.

1. Introduction

Sowmitras Technosoft Solutions ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to safeguarding your privacy. This Privacy Policy details how we collect, utilize, disclose, and secure your information when you interact with our website, services, and digital communications. By engaging with our services, you consent to the data practices described in this policy.

2. Information We Collect

We collect specific information to deliver and optimize our services. This data falls into three primary categories:

  • Directly Provided Data: Personal information such as your name, email address, phone number, and organizational details submitted voluntarily via contact forms, inquiries, or direct correspondence.
  • Automated Usage Data: Technical information gathered automatically through website analytics, including IP addresses, browser types, device specifications, and user interaction metrics.
  • Business Data: Confidential information and project specifications shared during formal consultations, project discovery phases, and ongoing service delivery.

3. Instagram and Meta Account Integrations

When you connect your professional Instagram or Meta accounts to our application, we request specific permissions to provide you with advanced analytics, management tools, and enhanced functionality. This collected data is kept strictly confidential, is never made public, and is exclusively accessible by you for your own analytical and operational purposes.

Depending on the features you use, we may request the following permissions:

  • Business Asset User Profile Access: To read user fields (such as ID, name, and profile picture) of individuals engaging with your business assets in order to provide customer insights.
  • Instagram Public Content Access: To access Instagram Graph API's hashtag search endpoints, enabling you to discover content associated with campaigns, gauge public sentiment, and securely manage your audience.
  • Instagram Business Basic: To retrieve foundational metadata from your Instagram Business account profile, including your username and ID.
  • Instagram Business Manage Messages: To facilitate viewing, managing, and responding to direct messages on your professional account, which may include integration with authorized third-party CRM tools.
  • Instagram Content Publish: To seamlessly manage your organic content creation process by enabling the authorized publication of photos and videos to your feed on your behalf.
  • Instagram Manage Engagement: To allow the authorized publishing or deletion of "Likes" on media objects (Feed or Reels) and comments on your behalf.
  • Instagram Manage Insights: To access comprehensive metadata, data insights, and story analytics for your linked Instagram Business account, and to discover profile information of other business profiles.
  • Public Profile Access: To authenticate you within our application and provide a personalized experience utilizing default public profile fields.

*Note: For all of the aforementioned Meta permissions, data may also be utilized in an aggregated, de-identified, or anonymized format to generate analytics that improve our application and support internal marketing efforts, provided that no individual user can be re-identified.

4. How We Use Your Information

We utilize the collected data strictly for professional purposes to deliver high-quality solutions and enhance the user experience. Specifically, we use your information to:

  • Fulfill service requests, process transactions, and respond promptly to client inquiries.
  • Communicate vital project updates, administrative notices, and contractual obligations.
  • Analyze website traffic and user behavior to optimize digital performance and functionality.
  • Maintain internal records for legal compliance, auditing, and operational efficiency.

5. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We firmly adhere to a policy of never selling or renting your personal information to third parties. We may, however, share your data with trusted service providers and corporate partners who assist us in operating our business and delivering services. All such third parties are bound by strict confidentiality agreements. Furthermore, we reserve the right to disclose your information if required to do so by law or in response to valid requests by public authorities.

6. Data Security Measures

We implement industry-standard administrative, technical, and physical security measures designed to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. While we strive to use commercially acceptable means to protect your data, no method of digital transmission or storage is entirely infallible.

7. Your Privacy Rights

Depending on your geographic jurisdiction, you may possess specific rights regarding your personal data. These generally include the right to access, rectify, restrict processing, or request the deletion of your personal information stored in our systems. You also retain the right to opt out of promotional communications at any time. To exercise these rights, please direct your request to our contact channels listed below.

Our digital platforms employ cookies and similar tracking technologies to analyze trends, administer the site, and track user navigation. These tools are essential for optimizing site functionality. You maintain the ability to control or reject the use of cookies at the individual browser level; however, doing so may limit your access to certain features of our website.
